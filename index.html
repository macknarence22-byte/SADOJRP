Welcome to SA DOJ Roleplay.

San Andreas Department of Justice Roleplay is a new FiveM community built on one principle — realism. We are a full server. Law enforcement, civilian life, Fire and EMS, criminal activity, and everything in between. Everyone has a role here and everyone is held to the same standard.

We are not a Hardcore Realism server. If you want serious, grounded roleplay with real command structure and a community that actually gives a damn about quality, this is the place.

San Andreas Department of Justice Roleplay is brand new and we are building this the right way from the ground up. That means the right people, the right culture, and the right foundation before anything else. Applications are open across all departments and civilian roles right now.

Community Requirements

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Speak and understand English
  • Own a legal copy of GTA V on PC
  • Have a capable PC, working headset, and microphone
  • Be willing to follow chain of command and server rules
  • Join our Discord and submit your application there

Law Enforcement Departments

Three agencies are active at launch. Each operates its own command structure and jurisdiction. The realism standard is the same across all three.

SASP
San Andreas State Police Statewide patrol, highway enforcement, and major incident command across all of San Andreas.
LSPD
Los Santos Police Department City policing, active urban response, and tight scene discipline inside Los Santos.
BCSO
Blaine County Sheriff’s Office County enforcement, rural calls, and open territory coverage across Blaine County.
